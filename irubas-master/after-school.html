Early education prepares a child for its future success. We, at iRUBAS aiming at establishing an exemplary chain of early learning centre by Inculcating Righteous & Ultimate Basic Academic Skills with a strong foundation.

We provide our children an enabling environments that will support learning and development at this crucial point and also fosters positive relationships. We aim to ensure, each and every day, that our children are cared for in a safe, nurturing and stimulating environment.

Our curriculum is Creative in-house pedagogy which is thematic based for each month with a focus on 3 primary areas.

  • Communication and Language
  • Physical Development
  • Personal, Social and Emotional Development

Our Syllabus has three main components.

  • Literacy
  • Mathematics
  • General Awareness
